20 years of ign game

This dataset is taken from kaggle. It contains the data of many games by ign.com from 1996 to 2016. This list of games is not exhaustive, however, as it does not contain all of the games from this period of time.

This data visualization supports the following interactions:

  • Highlight and tooltip by hovering the mouse over the area of the stacked area charts.
  • Tooltip by hovering the mouse over the bubble of the bubble chart.
  • Clickable legend of the area charts to filter which data to show on the area charts and which data to highlight on the bubble chart.
